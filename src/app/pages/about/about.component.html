Meet the Author

"Pascale writes in the realm with the giants. She is one step past fantastic that is hard to define, and a little frightening, and yet we yearn to travel there as often as possible"
--Adam Armstrong


Elaine Pascale has been writing for most of her life. She was born in Pennsylvania and grew up in rural parts of the state. For most of her young life, she lived in a valley that housed many myths, legends and cryptic creatures. She often sneaks and tweaks those myths into her own writing as they have had a lasting impression on her.
She then moved to Boston and later to Cape Cod. The Cape is the setting for her novella, The Blood Lights, and for The Thin Contract. Cape Cod is a beautiful place, but it becomes very isolated during the winters and that lends itself to some creepiness. She took a break from fiction in order to give birth to two children and a doctoral dissertation. She has taught a variety of courses from English Composition and Communications to a Vampire Seminar. Her writing has been published in several magazines and anthologies.
Now living in Florida she continues to write horror, and regularly appears on podcasts and participates in Women in Horror writing prompts.
